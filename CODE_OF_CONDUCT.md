# Code of Conduct

We follow the Contributor Covenant. By participating, you agree to uphold a respectful, inclusive community.

- Be kind and constructive. Assume good intent.
- No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Value different perspectives; be welcoming to newcomers.
- Give and accept feedback graciously.

Enforcement
- Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers (see SECURITY.md for contacts).
- Maintainers may take appropriate action, including warnings or removal from the community.
